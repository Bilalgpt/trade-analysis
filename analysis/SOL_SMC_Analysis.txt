HOW TO IDENTIFY LIQUIDITY — COMPLETE GUIDE
=====================================

STEP 1 — Mark Swing Highs and Lows
------------------------------------
- Every swing high = retail bulls put stops ABOVE = Buy-Side Liquidity (BSL)
- Every swing low  = retail bears put stops BELOW = Sell-Side Liquidity (SSL)
- Smart Money NEEDS this liquidity to fill their large orders

STEP 2 — Equal Highs/Lows = More Liquidity
-------------------------------------------
- If price touched same level 2+ times = more stop orders clustered there
- These are HIGH PROBABILITY sweep targets
- Look for them on 4H and Daily timeframes

STEP 3 — Recognize the Liquidity Sweep Signature
--------------------------------------------------
REAL sweep (reversal incoming):
  [+] Sharp spike THROUGH the level (wick, not body)
  [+] HIGH volume on that candle (smart money filling orders)
  [+] Candle CLOSES back above/below the level
  [+] Strong reversal candle follows immediately
  [+] RSI shows divergence at the sweep point

FAKE sweep / continuation:
  [-] Candle CLOSES beyond the level (real break = continuation)
  [-] Volume continues increasing after break
  [-] No reversal — price just keeps going

STEP 4 — Confirm with CHoCH (Change of Character)
---------------------------------------------------
- After SSL sweep → price must BREAK a recent swing high on 1H or 4H
- That break = Change of Character (CHoCH) = Smart Money reversed
- Enter on RETEST of that broken level (now acts as support)
- Do NOT enter on the sweep candle itself — wait for confirmation

STEP 5 — Time Entries with Sessions
-------------------------------------
- London Open  (08:00–10:00 UTC): Most liquidity sweeps happen here
- New York Open (13:00–15:00 UTC): Confirms or reverses London move
- Highest institutional order flow in these windows
- Avoid entries during Asian session (low volume, fake moves)
